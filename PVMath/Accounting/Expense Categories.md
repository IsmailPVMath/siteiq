# Expense Categories (PVMath)

For bookkeeping (Excel, Lexoffice, sevDesk, or Steuerberater). Use consistent categories from day one.

---

## Revenue (Einnahmen)

| Category | Examples |
|----------|----------|
| SaaS subscriptions | Stripe Professional / Developer |
| Pilot / proforma | Bank transfer before Stripe |
| Enterprise | Custom invoices |

---

## Cost of delivery (direct)

| Category | Examples | Notes |
|----------|----------|-------|
| Hosting | Railway, Supabase paid tier | Core infra |
| Maps / APIs | Paid geocoding if added | Currently mostly free OSM/PVGIS |
| Email | Brevo | OTP + transactional |
| Payment fees | Stripe % + fixed | Deductible business expense |

---

## Software & tools (Betriebsausgaben)

| Category | Examples |
|----------|----------|
| Dev tools | Cursor, GitHub, domain registrar (Namecheap) |
| Design / docs | Canva, Microsoft 365 if used for business |
| Accounting | Steuerberater, Lexoffice/sevDesk |

## Marketing & sales

| Category | Examples |

## Professional & legal

| Category | Examples |
|----------|----------|
| Steuerberater | Formation, monthly bookkeeping |
| Legal | Terms review, AVV template (future) |
| IHK | Mandatory chamber fees |

---

## Equipment (usually AfA if > €800 net)

| Category | Examples |
|----------|----------|
| Hardware | Laptop used for PVMath (allocate % business use with Steuerberater) |
| Phone | Business call portion |

---

## Not business expenses

- Personal meals without client presence
- Fines / penalties
- Private health insurance (special rules — ask Steuerberater)

---

## Receipt rules

- Keep PDF/email for every expense
- Note business purpose on travel (e.g. “Intersolar Munich — customer demos”)
- Separate business bank transactions where possible
