YieldIQ — yield, PR & GCR

Compare four ground-mount configurations early — before PVsyst or lender-grade modelling.

Four configurations

YieldIQ runs PVGIS for 1P Fixed, 2P Fixed, 1P Tracker, and 2P Tracker at your chosen GCR and loss assumptions. Results support configuration choice in pre-feasibility — typically ±8–15% versus bankable studies depending on site and assumptions.

Key outputs

  • Specific yield — kWh/kWp/year; normalizes sites for fair comparison
  • POA irradiance — plane-of-array resource for each mount type
  • Performance ratio (PR) — system efficiency including cumulative losses
  • Capacity factor (CF) — annual utilization of nameplate DC capacity
  • Shading & total loss — row shading from GCR plus PVGIS physics derates

Ground coverage ratio (GCR)

GCR is module collection area divided by ground footprint. Higher GCR increases DC density but raises inter-row shading. YieldIQ models shading from your GCR sliders; trackers include backtracking benefit versus fixed tilt at the same GCR.

SiteIQ / TopoIQ capacity screening uses a standard GCR band for MWp/ha estimates — separate from the GCR you set in YieldIQ for energy comparison.

Screening vs analysis profile

SiteIQ uses a lighter PVGIS screening profile for speed in combined reports. YieldIQ uses disclosed soiling, other losses, and GCR shading for a richer analysis profile. Both are preliminary — not substitutes for PVsyst, independent engineer review, or lender models.

Temperature loss in YieldIQ comes from PVGIS physics — not a flat percentage guess. Total loss reflects PVGIS combined derates where available.
