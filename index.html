Data Sources & Accuracy

Where the data comes from — and what it's good for.

PVGIS — EC Joint Research Centre
EU Commission's satellite irradiance database. Used in bankable pre-feasibility studies under IEC 61724 globally. GHI uncertainty: ~5–8% at P50 level depending on region. Same source referenced by major independent engineers (DNV, BV, TÜV).
Copernicus GLO-30 DEM — ESA / Airbus
30 m horizontal resolution global DEM from TanDEM-X radar imagery. Vertical accuracy ~1–4 m RMSE in open terrain. TopoIQ uses this for slope maps, contour extraction, and DXF or LandXML terrain files. Suitable for site selection and conceptual layout — not a substitute for RTK survey.
⚠ YieldIQ — Know the Limits
YieldIQ uses PVGIS satellite data with simplified shading models. Typical uncertainty vs. a full bankable energy yield assessment: ±8–15%. Use for internal go/no-go screening only. For financing, permitting, or EPC contracts: commission a bankable assessment from a certified independent engineer (PVsyst + P50/P90 analysis).
