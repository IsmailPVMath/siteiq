PVGIS — EC Joint Research Centre

EU Commission irradiance database (SARAH / ERA5). GHI uncertainty ~5–8% at P50. Same source used in bankable pre-feasibility globally.

Region-Routed Terrain DEM

Best public DEM per location: Copernicus EEA-10 (Europe, ~10 m), USGS 3DEP (USA, ~10 m), FABDEM bare-earth (global, ~30 m), Copernicus GLO-30 fallback. Resampled to a 5 m layout grid — not RTK survey.

⚠ YieldIQ — Know the Limits

PVGIS with simplified shading. Typical uncertainty vs. bankable: ±8–15%. Internal go/no-go only.

FAQ

Common questions from engineers.

What PVMath is — and isn’t — in a ground-mount workflow.

How is PVMath different from running PVGIS and QGIS yourself?

Same datasets — one workflow. Screening, terrain PDFs, CAD export, and four-config yield comparison in ≤ 4 minutes.

Is this a bankable study or a substitute for PVsyst / DNV / Aurora?

No. Pre-feasibility screening for go/no-go. YieldIQ: ±8–15% vs. bankable. TerrainIQ: region-routed public DEM, not LiDAR.

What does Early Access mean?

The full platform is live today. Features and pricing may evolve. Free tier includes PDF and CAD export.

Can I use TerrainIQ exports in CAD for layout?

Yes — for early layout. TerrainIQ delivers a UTM LandXML surface and DXF contours with parcel linework from your boundary. Screening-grade public DEM — verify critical grades with survey before FEED.

Who is PVMath built for?

Solar EPCs, developers, and engineering firms screening utility-scale ground mount globally. Fixed tilt and tracker, standard and Agri-PV.
