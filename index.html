Who We Are

Built by solar engineers,
for solar engineers.

PVMath was founded by a utility-scale solar engineer with several years of hands-on experience in tracker systems, site design, and EPC project execution across Europe and beyond.

We know the pain points because we lived them — slow feasibility studies, BOM errors from layout revisions, procurement delays, and manual data transfer between teams. PVMath automates and accelerates all of it.

Our platform is designed for the professionals who build the world's solar infrastructure: EPCs, project developers, and engineering firms who need fast, reliable, bankable data.

MODULE 02
Live Now

TopoIQ

Satellite terrain extraction for solar site engineering. Copernicus DEM processed to Civil 3D-ready formats — LandXML, DXF contours, XYZ point clouds — with slope and elevation analysis.

  • Copernicus GLO-30 DEM · ~2.4m accuracy
  • Draw boundary on satellite map
  • LandXML TIN surface for Civil 3D
  • DXF contours — major & minor intervals
  • XYZ point cloud (Easting/Northing/Elev)
  • Slope map — solar-grade colour scale
  • PDF terrain intelligence report

How It Works

Site screening in
three steps.

1

Locate Your Site

Search by name, paste coordinates (lat/lon), drop a pin on the Google Satellite map, or upload a KML / KMZ / DXF boundary file directly.

2

Set Project Parameters

Choose Standard or Agri-PV land use, Fixed Tilt or Single-Axis Tracker system, and enter your project name and target country.

3

Get Your Intelligence Report

Solar resource, terrain slope, flood risk, regulatory authority contacts, capacity estimate — all in under 60 seconds. PDF report included, ready for client meetings.
