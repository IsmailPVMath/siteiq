2. What the Platform Does

PVMath provides an integrated software platform for ground-mount solar site screening and engineering support: project setup, solar resource and regulatory screening (SiteIQ), terrain analysis (TerrainIQ), layout generation (LayoutIQ), yield screening (YieldIQ), and economic screening (RevenueIQ), plus exportable reports and terrain/CAD files (PDF, DXF, LandXML). Outputs are generated from third-party data sources (PVGIS, Copernicus/SRTM DEM, OpenStreetMap) and automated calculations.

The Platform provides preliminary, early-stage screening outputs only. It is a decision-support tool for the early phases of project development and does not replace a bankable energy yield assessment, geotechnical survey, RTK/topographic survey, or detailed engineering design by a qualified professional.
