# PVMath Marketing Strategy 2026

**Position:** Solar Site Intelligence Platform for ground-mount EPCs and developers.  
**Tagline:** From site to system.  
**Not:** A hype AI startup, bankability consultant, or LiDAR replacement.

---

## 1. Strategic objective

Build **trusted technical authority** with senior EPC decision-makers in **DE · ES · IN · GCC** — enough to drive:

1. **Free trial signups** (siteiq.pvmath.com)  
2. **Professional upgrades** (€149/mo)  
3. **Inbound from civil / development teams** who need CAD handoff and screening speed  

Success metrics (track monthly):

| Metric | Target (6 mo) |
|--------|----------------|
| LinkedIn impressions (company + founder) | Baseline → +50% |
| siteiq.pvmath.com signups | Track UTM `linkedin`, `guides` |
| Knowledge Centre sessions | ↑ via post CTAs |
| Inbound contact@ emails mentioning screening/CAD | Qualitative |
| Free → Pro conversion | Track in Supabase |

---

## 2. ICP (ideal customer profile)

**Primary:** Utility-scale ground-mount **EPC / project developer / civil subcontractor**  
- 20–500 MWp pipeline  
- Fixed tilt + tracker + Agri-PV  
- Pain: KMZ → screening → LiDAR decision → CAD starter geometry  

**Secondary:** Engineering firms doing **owner’s engineer screening** for investors  

**Not primary:** Residential, rooftop installers, pure finance without engineering gate  

---

## 3. Positioning statement

> PVMath compresses the first weeks of greenfield screening — solar, terrain, and yield configuration — into one project setup and three modules. Screening-grade outputs engineers can defend; survey and bankable studies when the site earns them.

### Differentiators (say often)

| Us | Typical alternative |
|----|---------------------|
| One Project Setup → SiteIQ + TopoIQ + YieldIQ | Disconnected GIS + Excel + manual PVsyst |
| Honest GLO-30 (~30 m) vs 5 m grid disclosure | Oversold “high-res DEM” |
| LandXML/DXF + **SITE_BOUNDARY** parcel linework | KMZ-only handoff |
| Cross-row slope for trackers | Mean slope only |
| Knowledge Centre + Pro manual (no IP leak) | Black-box “AI scores” |

### Proof points (always citable)

- PVGIS (JRC), Copernicus GLO-30, OpenStreetMap  
- Screening disclaimers in every PDF  
- pvmath.com/guides for public engineering education  

---

## 4. Message hierarchy

**Level 1 — Platform (pvmath.com)**  
Screening → terrain → yield. Global. Freemium.

**Level 2 — Module**  
- SiteIQ: go/no-go PDF, PVMath Score (screening only)  
- TopoIQ: terrain + CAD before LiDAR  
- YieldIQ: fixed vs tracker at your GCR  

**Level 3 — Feature / guide**  
Cross-row, GLO-30 vs 5 m, LandXML workflow, screening vs survey  

Never lead with Level 3 on cold audience; lead with pain (Level 1 hook) then Level 2 proof.
